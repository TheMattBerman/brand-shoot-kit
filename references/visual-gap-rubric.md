# Visual Gap Rubric

Use this to classify current product-page visuals.

## Status Scale

- `Present and strong`: conversion-useful and on-brand
- `Present but weak`: exists but low clarity/trust/utility
- `Missing`: should exist for this product but absent
- `Not relevant`: genuinely unnecessary for this category

## Priority Rules

- High: likely improves conversion or reduces confusion
- Medium: meaningful support asset, not critical
- Low: campaign/seasonal or optional variation

## Minimum Coverage Targets

- PDP hero + clean background + alternate angle
- one human scale/in-use shot where relevant
- one texture/detail/feature proof shot
- one channel-ready social or email adaptation

## Anti-Patterns

- marking everything as High
- forcing model shots for all categories
- recommending novelty shots before trust shots
