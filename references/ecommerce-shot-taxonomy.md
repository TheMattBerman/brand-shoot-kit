# Ecommerce Shot Taxonomy

## PDP / Commerce

- hero front
- white/neutral background
- alternate angle
- scale context
- detail texture
- bundle/group

## Lifestyle

- product in natural environment
- usage moment
- routine scene (morning/evening/workout/etc)

## Model / Human

- hand hold/use
- body crop application
- full-face scene only when necessary

## Channel Adaptations

- social square (1:1)
- social vertical (9:16)
- email/home hero (16:9)
- marketplace safe image (1:1, clean background)
