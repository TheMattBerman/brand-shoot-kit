# Ecommerce Shot Taxonomy

## PDP / Commerce Sequence

- clean hero front (conversion-first, high legibility)
- alternate angle with label/detail readability
- bundle/contents layout for SKU clarity
- texture/ingredient/supporting props detail proof
- hand-held scale proof
- human/lifestyle product-in-use scene
- white/neutral marketplace-safe image

## Lifestyle / Human

- product in natural environment with believable set logic
- usage moment tied to category routine
- hand-held or body-crop in-use framing when relevant
- avoid generic "product centered in empty void" styling

## Scale Guidance

- hero/marketplace shots: dominant product framing
- detail shots: tighter crop on label/surface/ingredient signals
- hand-held/in-use shots: moderate product size with realistic human anatomy
- lifestyle/email/story shots: smaller product footprint with intentional context and negative space
- bundle shots: primary SKU clear, supporting items subordinate

## Channel Adaptations

- social square (1:1)
- social vertical (9:16)
- email/home hero (16:9)
- marketplace safe image (1:1, clean background)
- every channel crop should preserve product truth first, then optimize composition for placement
