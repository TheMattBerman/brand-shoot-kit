# QA Rubric

| Criterion | Weight | Pass Rule |
|---|---:|---|
| Product accuracy | 30 | Label, shape, color, and count are correct |
| Commerce usefulness | 20 | Image helps buyer understand or trust product |
| Brand fit | 15 | Tone/palette matches brand profile |
| Scene realism | 15 | Lighting, hands, props are believable |
| Visual clarity | 10 | Product is clearly visible and legible |
| Artifact risk | 10 | No obvious generation defects |

## Scoring

- 85+: approve
- 70-84: usable with caution
- 50-69: reroll
- <50: reject

## Auto-Reject

- changed label text/logo
- wrong product geometry/count
- fake claims/certifications
- malformed hands touching product
- product too obscured for commerce use
