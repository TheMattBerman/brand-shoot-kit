# Generation Prompts

## Shot 01 - Clean front tub hero

**Use case:** PDP
**Aspect ratio:** 1:1

**Prompt:**
Create a 1:1 ecommerce image for Daily Greens Superblend (Peak Harvest) optimized for PDP. Shot intent: Clean front tub hero. Scale guidance: clean hero framing; product fills 62-75% with immediate label readability. Human guidance: no human subject required; keep focus on pack fidelity and commerce clarity. Context guidance: controlled ecommerce set with minimal props and clean background separation. Brand tone: brand-consistent ecommerce. Scene direction: Use kitchen or desk routine context with shaker, water, or scoop cues. Keep claims presentation credible. Evidence anchors from source: scoop, powder, label. Must preserve exactly: tub silhouette, front claims, supplement facts label. Never change: nutrition claims, servings text, brand name. Watch distortion risks: claim text drift, scoop scale mismatch, label warp. Keep props supportive, never dominant.

**Negative constraints:**
- no altered logo or label text
- no fake ingredient/claim badges
- no malformed hands or impossible anatomy
- no product count mismatch for intended SKU
- no prop occlusion hiding the primary label

**Reroll if failed:**
If label legibility, pack geometry, or product count fails, reroll with flatter camera angle, cleaner background, and tighter product framing.
