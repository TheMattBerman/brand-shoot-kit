# Generation Prompts

## Shot 01 - Front hero clean

**Use case:** PDP
**Aspect ratio:** 1:1

**Prompt:**
Create a 1:1 ecommerce product image for Daily Greens Powder. Keep package geometry, brand name, and label text unchanged. Scene intent: Front hero clean. Match Daily Harvest Labs tone (credible and energetic). Product is the visual anchor.

**Negative constraints:**
- no altered logo or label text
- no extra products unless shot requires bundle
- no malformed hands
- no fake badges/certifications

**Reroll if failed:**
If label or product geometry drifts, reroll with camera closer, flatter angle, and reduced background complexity.
