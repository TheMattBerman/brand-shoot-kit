# Output Quality Rubric

Score each packet 1-5 on:

1. Brand diagnosis quality
2. Product preservation clarity
3. Gap audit prioritization accuracy
4. Shoot strategy specificity
5. Shot list practicality (not bloated)
6. Prompt operational quality
7. QA rigor (reject logic)
8. Export mapping usefulness
9. Graceful degradation behavior
10. Boundary discipline (no ad-spy drift)

## Passing Bar

- Average >= 4.0
- No criterion below 3
- Automatic fail if product preservation is weak or ad-intelligence scope creeps in
